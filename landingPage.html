Getting Started with Drums
Master the basics with confidence
Learn how to hold sticks properly, sit with good posture, and play your first grooves on the snare and hi-hat. Perfect for total beginners, younger students, or anyone returning to drums after a break.
  • Play along to 2–3 basic songs
  • Understand your gear and setup
  • Keep a steady beat using both hands

Timing, Tempo & Rudiments
Sharpen your skills and sound tighter
Drummers are timekeepers — and this section trains your ear, hands, and coordination with essential rudiments and tempo drills.

More About Mark

Hi, I'm Mark Proctor — a lifelong drummer and passionate teacher based in Claremore, Oklahoma. I picked up my first pair of sticks at age 9 and haven't stopped since.

For over a decade, I've been helping students of all ages build skill, confidence, and a love for music. Whether you're a complete beginner or looking to refine your technique, my lessons are personalized to your goals — and always rooted in patience, encouragement, and rhythm.

I've played everything from rock and country to jazz, worship, and marching band. Over the years, I've had the privilege of performing with local groups, teaching in schools, and watching students grow into confident players both on and off the stage.

My mission is simple: make learning drums fun, approachable, and rewarding — no matter your age or experience level.

Let's find your groove together.
